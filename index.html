P5 Simulation

Above is a natural molecule simulation that was designed and developed using the P5 javascript library. When the infected molecules intersect with the healthy molecules they have a 40% chance of infecting them. Use the sliders in the controllers at the top right of the screen to adjust some elements, such as the size of the molecules, how many appear on the
